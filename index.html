Vehicle Transformer

Game Introduction

User will start with a bike, when moving forward, user has chance to eat coins to get credits. There are moving explosive cars and black pitfalls on the road. Player should try to avoid meeting them. Or player will die.

Choose Game Level

There are 3 levels of difficulty in this game.

Easy

  • Designed for the beginner
  • Not many pitfalll or explosive cars in this level
  • Player could level up easily by collecting coins

Medium

  • Designed for the intermediate player
  • More pitfall and explosive car will appear
  • The speed of the explosive car will be increased, so it is more difficult for player to avoid it

Hard

  • Designed for veteran for game
  • There will be pitfalls all over the road
  • Frequency of explosive cars are high
  • Player is hard to gain more points
